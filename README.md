

# Meat Consumption & Sustainable Practices in Kyrgyzstan
## Minor in Environmental and Scientific Literacy Capstone Project

### Project Overview
Investigating meat consumption patterns and traditional sustainable practices in Kyrgyzstan through survey analysis (n=51).

**Author**: BEGIMBAEVA Alima Nur
**Institution**: Lingnan University  
**Supervisor**: GENG Hongyan Helen  
**Date**: November 2025

### Research Questions
1. What are current meat consumption patterns in Kyrgyzstan?
2. How are traditional sustainable practices integrated into household systems?
3. Is there a relationship between environmental awareness and willingness to reduce consumption?
4. How can these practices be scaled?

### Data & Methods
- **Sample Size**: 51 respondents
- **Survey Type**: Mixed-methods (quantitative + qualitative)
- **Analysis Methods**:
